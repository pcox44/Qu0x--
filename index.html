How to Play

  • Use all five dice exactly once each to build an expression.
  • Allowed operations: +, -, *, /, ^, (, ), and ! (factorial).
  • Click dice and operators to build your expression.
  • Expression evaluates in real time.
  • Submit to see your score: difference from target number.
  • Get a perfect score (0 difference) to Qu0x and lock that day!
  • Once locked, you cannot modify that day's expression.
